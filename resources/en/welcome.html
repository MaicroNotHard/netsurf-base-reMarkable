Welcome to NetSurf-reMarkable

This is an adaptation of the open-source web browser NetSurf for the reMarkable. It supports pen and touch input, and changes to scrolling and navigation designed for a great experience on the reMarkable's eInk display.

What works, and what doesn't? For a list of currently known bugs and open feature requests, visit the issues page on GitHub. New suggestions or bug reports are also welcome!
